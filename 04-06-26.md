Answer 

When SpringApplication.run() is called, Spring Boot creates an ApplicationContext, performs component scanning, loads configuration properties, executes auto-configuration based on classpath and conditions, creates and wires beans through dependency injection, applies bean post-processors and proxies, starts the embedded web server, registers DispatcherServlet, and finally publishes startup events to make the application ready to serve requests.
